FANTASY, you say?
Fantasy fiction involves any story that could not happen in real life. Many times, this involves epic world building, magical systems, or bizarre species.

High Fantasy takes place in a world other than our own. Think Lord of the Rings or Throne of Glass.
CONTEMPORARY?
The heck?
Contemporary fiction takes place in our world, and portrays a realistic story. Contemporary authors populate our world with imaginary characters and situations that reflect our society.
What the heck
is Science Fiction anyway?
Science Fiction is often confused with fantasy. However, sci-fi is not wholly imaginary and must incorporate existing or fictionalized scientific principles.
